Places to Eat

Your trip to Belagavi can get even more wholesome by trying their local cuisine. Here are some eateries that provide the best in terms of authentic flavors, unique dining experiences, and unforgettable meals.

Niyaaz

Niyaaz is renowned for its authentic Belagavi biryani crafted with fragrant Basmati rice, tender chunks of meat, and a blend of traditional spices and vegetables. Popular dishes: Belagavi biryani and Kababs.

New Grand Restaurant

New Grand Restaurant offers diverse North Indian and South Indian delicacies in a charming setting. Indulge in their delicious food while enjoying their excellent service and inviting ambience. Popular dishes: Dosa, Idli, and crunchy kurma poori.

Tumkur Tatte Idli

Tumkur Tatte Idli is a popular destination for street food enthusiasts. Their signature dish, the thatte idli, is a must-try, offering a unique and delicious culinary experience. Popular dishes: Thatte idli.

Paratha Corner

For lovers of North Indian cuisine, Paratha Corner is a must-visit. It provides an extensive menu featuring a variety of parathas with different fillings. Popular dishes: Aloo paratha and paneer paratha.

Ajanta Cafe

Ajanta Cafe is one of the most prominent vegetarian restaurants in Belagavi, where you can try several different cuisines all in one place. Popular dishes: Chhole bhatoore, dosa, and puri bhaji.

Eats n Treats

Eats n Treats is a popular restaurant known for its delicious food and comfortable ambience. It is known for its quality food, generous portions, and friendly service. Popular dishes: Papritos pizza, quattro formaggi pizza, and cheese chilli toast.

Gokak Falls
A breathtaking waterfall known for its picturesque setting, it is historically significant as a source of hydroelectric power.
Experience Time: 1-2 hours
Belagavi Fort
Belgaum Fort testifies to the city's rich history and architectural prowess. It offers a glimpse into the region's diverse cultural heritage.
Experience Time: 1-2 hours
Kamal Basti
Kamal Basti is a Jain temple known for its exquisite carving, intricate architecture, and religious significance.
Experience Time: 1-2 hours
Military Shri Mahadev Temple
Located on the Camp Road in Belgaum in Karnataka, Military Mahadev Temple is one of the prime attractions in the city that has been built and maintained by the Indian Army.
Experience Time: 1-2 hours
Survarna Soudha
Suvarna Soudha stands as a symbol of Karnataka's governance and is a popular tourist attraction due to its grandeur and historical significance. It is situated at Khanapur Road, Belagavi, Karnataka, India.
Kittur Fort and palace
It is a historic site known for its association with the Rani Chennamma, a legendary queen who fought against the British in the 19th century. The fort, built by the Marathas, was the center of resistance during her reign.

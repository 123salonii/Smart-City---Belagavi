Discover The Glorious Cascade

Belagavi is home to stunning waterfalls, including the picturesque Gokak Falls and the serene Magod Falls. These natural wonders offer breathtaking views and are perfect spots for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Tilari Waterfall

The closest waterfalls to the city of Belgaum, the Tilari Falls are a true gift of nature. The emerald waters of the waterfalls, and the short plunge give make the whole place absolutely picture perfect. Swimming is a popular activity here and those who love water cannot resist jumping in the clean, emerald pool formed at the bottom of the waterfall.

Kalasa Falls

Located a short distance from Shimbola Falls, the Kalasa falls are more of a mainstream tourist destination. The reason is that this waterfall is accessible by road and there is no hiking involved. However, less effort in reaching the waterfall says nothing about its amazing beauty. The waterfall is just as picturesque as any other on this list and offers a special advantage: the freedom to take a dip in the fresh water!.

Shimbola Waterfalls

Discovered rather recently, Shimbola Falls are accessible after a hike through the surrounding forest area. The hike distance is 12 kilometers and can take upto 5 hours.With that said, the hiking experience itself is superb. The trail goes through lush green forest and is dotted with awe inspiring views throughout the 12 kilometer stretch. At the end, the beauty of the waterfall will make matters even more magical.

VajraPoha Waterfall

Often lovingly called the gift of nature, the VajraPoha is a might waterfall with a drop height exceeding 600 feet. Thanks to this unreal height, the waterfall gets a dauntingly beautiful image that attracts hundreds of travelers. While witnessing the roaring waterfall and spending a few hours near the streams is a popular activity, the same should be undertaken with great caution, especially during the monsoon months when the flow of the water is relentless.
